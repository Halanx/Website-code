About Us

Bringing our entire neighborhood within thumbs reach, and so building a connected, safer, affordable, and convenient neighborhood.

Through Halanx, people get to know what’s going on in their neighborhood from their neighbors like

  • Crime & safety alerts
  • Local news
  • Cultural events
  • Lost pets
  • Best plumber, electrician, etc

And they get cashback on shopping bills at local businesses like restaurants, grocery stores, pharmacies, and more, and use them to save more on deals at local businesses.

This way, we help local businesses grow by bringing them new customers, and retaining the existing ones.

So we’re on the mission to make our neighborhoods safer and stronger, while creating meaningful work for
underemployed people, helping local businesses grow, and bringing affordable convenience to consumers.
